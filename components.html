Image
We have responsive images which can switch according to the resolution of the display or density of the display or the art direction.

Density switching

Density switching allows the website to switch across differnent DPI.

For example our website will load very good image on good display and average quality image on low display

Art Direction

We can switch the images based on the art direction that is landscape or protrait

Resolution switching

We can switch images from the data base of images according to the resolution of the display.

Lazy image Loading

Using this attribute we will only load those images when that image is on view port.
